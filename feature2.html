AI-controlled systems refer to systems or devices that use artificial intelligence (AI) algorithms to make decisions, control processes, and perform tasks autonomously. How is the AI CONTROLLED works?

AI-controlled systems use various techniques and algorithms to make decisions and control processes autonomously. These methods depend on the system and its intended application, but generally involve machine learning, deep learning, and natural language processing (NLP). Machine learning involves training the AI system using large amounts of data, allowing it to identify patterns and make predictions or decisions based on that data. Different types of machine learning algorithms are used, including supervised learning, unsupervised learning, and reinforcement learning. Deep learning is a subset of machine learning that uses neural networks to process and analyze data. It is effective for tasks such as image and speech recognition, where large amounts of data and complex patterns need to be processed. NLP trains the AI system to understand and process human language, enabling it to perform tasks such as language translation, chatbot interactions, and voice assistants

    Once trained, AI-controlled systems can operate autonomously, making decisions and controlling processes based on input from sensors, data streams, or other sources. These systems may incorporate feedback loops, allowing them to learn and adapt to changing conditions over time. The specific algorithms and techniques used will depend on the desired performance objectives and the type of data available.
